Lopez Jaena

In the heart of the simple Municipality of Lopez Jaena lies the breathtaking beauty of Kapayas Island. A rocky islet full of wonder and excitement. Its glistening waters waiting for you to take a plunge. Be enthralled and enjoy a refreshing summer in Kapayas Island!

Sapang Dalaga

Sapang Dalaga is a quiet coastal town in the northern part of Misamis Occidental. The placid waters of Casul Bay hems its shore while Mount Dasa & Pedoluan frames it on land. As the town thrives mainly on agri & aqua cultures, fresh vegetable & seafood meals are guaranteed gastronomic treats.

Aloran

Aloran is a coastal municipality in the province of Misamis Occidental. The municipality has a land area of 118.06 square kilometers or 45.58 square miles which constitutes 5.88% of Misamis Occidental's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 27,934.

Panaon

Panaon is a coastal municipality in the province of Misamis Occidental. The municipality has a land area of 46.80 square kilometers or 18.07 square miles which constitutes 2.33% of Misamis Occidental's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 10,797.

Jimenez

Jimenez is a coastal municipality in the province of Misamis Occidental. The municipality has a land area of 81.43 square kilometers or 31.44 square miles which constitutes 4.06% of Misamis Occidental's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 28,909.

Ozamis

Ozamiz is a coastal component city in the province of Misamis Occidental. The city has a land area of 169.95 square kilometers or 65.62 square miles which constitutes 8.47% of Misamis Occidental's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 140,334.

Bonifacio

Bonifacio is a landlocked municipality in the coastal province of Misamis Occidental.The municipality has a land area of 155.02 square kilometers or 59.85 square miles which constitutes 7.73% of Misamis Occidental's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 34,558.
