Aloran

Explore the mysterious twists and turns of Aloran's Snake River. Experience an unforgetable ride thru the lush and beautiful scenery. Sometime in 1854 Anuran was visited by an Agustinian priest named Francisco Jimenez de San Fermin. He built a small chapel. His congregation eventually grow and in 1885 with the inauguration of the Catholic Church Parish, Anuran was renamed Aloran and became a municipality, part of huge province of Cagayan de Misamis.
